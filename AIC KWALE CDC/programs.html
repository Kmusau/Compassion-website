Sponsor visit

During one of our sponsor visits. Sometimes we are priviledged to host a sponsor when he/she decides to pay a visit to the beneficiary they support. Its always a good time for us and moreso the beneficiary. 

Survival Intervention Program

We continue to stretch our hands now even to expectant mothers and upto their children are 3 years of age. Its a stage where a lot is required to bring up a child and also ensure they have the right medical care and nutrition therefore reducing infant mortality in our society.

Youth corner

Youths in the project meet and discuss different agendas concerning them. They share out their ideas and get supported. They also have zoom meetings with other youths in the country to also exchange ideas.
