On Going Projects

Currently we have a couple of projects which are in progress and we strongly believe that they will be of great importance to us once completed

Youth projects

Youths being the leaders of tommorrow; we try to empower and equip them whenever they come up with a good initiative. They are also given a platform to exchange ideas and present them to higher authorities 

Classroom construction

We saw the need of building some other rooms where most of our activities will be taking place in. This will make our service delivery efficient and the classrooms can also be used to teach the young ones

Tailoring classes

We are having tailoring classes on going mainly for the caregivers. This gives them a skill which they can use it to earn a living. All this is targeted to improving their living standards

Why we stand out

Our beneficiaries are always proud of us because of what we offer here. This makes them feel like they own the project

Spiritual growth

Being a church sponsored organization, in everything that we do we put God first. Thats why we teach our beneficiaries spiritual matters

Youth empowerment

When the children grow and start to become youths, we give them lessons that fit them and help them in the outside world

Health

Each year we make sure that every child is done a heath screening to know how they are developing and know if they have any underlying conditions

Reinstate

Like Jesus came to seek the lost, we also try to bring our beneficiaries back to their initial state in such a case of neglect or abuse. 

Relieving from poverty

Compassion international slogan being Relieving children from poverty. Thats also our main agenda here at AIC Kwale CDC

Talents

We support different talents in our beneficiaries by helding competitions in games, soft talents or any other talents they have like singing and acting.
