About us

AIC Kwale is a christian church located in the Makueni county along Nairobi-Mombasa road. AIC Kwale is a Child Development Centre (CDC) patnering with Compassion International to reach the less fortunate children in the society. It works towards molding a child from the tender ages to be a morally fit person in the society.
We try to achieve our goals through the different programs, initiatives and teachings that we give to our sponsored beneficiaries. 

Spiritual Development 

Proverbs 22:6 “Train a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not turn from it.”
We give our beneficiaries Biblical teachings so that they can grow to be a good example in the society 

Cognitive Development

A prize giving day at our project.
We value and support child’s academic life and also try to assist with facilities like our library, giving them textbooks and also paying some of their school fees.

Social-emotional Domain

Some of our beneficiaries on an exposure trip to the Kenyatta University main campus. This helps break class monotonuos and also improve their healthy interaction with different people in the society.

Food Security 

Caregivers being provided with Sprayers to help fight pests and diseases which their crops and animals in order to get maximum produce from their agricultural practices

Technology

Students studying computer practical skills. As the world turns digital we are not being left behind and therefore we see the need to tequip these students with computer skills

Beneficiaries graduating from CSP to CDSP
